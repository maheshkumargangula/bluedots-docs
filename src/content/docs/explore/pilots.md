Two district pilots — in **Ghaziabad** (Uttar Pradesh) and **Dharwad** (Karnataka) — set out to answer a precise question. They did **not** test whether local demand and supply existed. They tested whether that demand and supply could be made **visible** and **actionable**.

The answer was unambiguous. The pilots **revealed**, rather than created, demand and supply that was always there.

## Hidden demand, surfaced

When roughly **10% of SMBs** in each district were Blue Dotted, **over 10,000 local job openings surfaced per district** — many of which had been circulating through word-of-mouth and fragmented postings for months. During the same period, leading national platforms showed **fewer than 100** active listings for the same districts.

> The demand was never absent. It was digitally dark.

## Supply, structured in two weeks

In each of Dharwad and Ghaziabad, **over 15,000 job seekers** surfaced and were structured into discoverable digital signals in just **two weeks** — **20,000+ across the two districts**.

Voice-enabled Blue Dotting reduced intent capture to **2–3 minutes per person**, replacing physical camps, job fairs and intermediary networks that previously took weeks of mobilisation.

## Outcomes

- Placement conversion rose from **under 10%** at typical job fairs to **over 50%** in targeted placement drives.
- Time to discover a relevant candidate fell from **more than 2 weeks to under 2 days**.
- Jobs that once required long commutes — often to another city — were now **within 2 bus stops**.

## Before / After

| Dimension | Before | After |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Job openings visible locally | < 100 on national platforms | 10,000+ per district, from < 10% of SMBs |
| Cost of capturing local economic intelligence | ₹500+ per interaction (traditional field survey) | < ₹10 per interaction (voice AI) |
| Time to become digitally discoverable | Multiple touchpoints, physical presence required | 2–3-minute voice conversation on any phone |
| Speed of surfacing job seekers | Weeks of physical mobilisation, camps and job fairs | 20,000+ surfaced across two districts in 2 weeks |
| Time to discover a relevant candidate | > 2 weeks | < 2 days |
| Placement conversion | < 10% at job fairs | > 50% in targeted placement drives |
| Distance to employment | Long commutes, often to another city | Within 2 bus stops |

## Named proof

> Shri Abhinav Gopal, IAS, Chief Development Officer, Ghaziabad, outlined how job and candidate aggregators come together to generate Blue Dots across the district through a **Jobs Facilitation Centre (JFC)**.

In **Dharwad**, manufacturers were convened by a local SMB association to convert their job openings into Blue Dots, and electrician graduates from **ITI Dharwad** were made digitally discoverable.

## Ecosystem uptake

A range of organisations are already leveraging Blue Dots — placement and skilling organisations sharpening their targeting, assessment providers, and local operators scaling deployment. Named participants from the pilots include:

- Head Held High
- Recex
- JobsUFL
- TRRAIN
- Proof Of Skill
- Digital Labour Chowk

## Momentum

Both districts are expanding the approach internally. **Uttar Pradesh and Karnataka are scaling it to multiple districts across each state in the coming year.**
