## Solving the paradox of proximity

Cabs and riders find each other in minutes. Livelihoods and talent, just bus stops
apart, cannot. The difference is not distance — it is **digital discoverability**.

A **Blue Dot** is a local signal of intent from a citizen or enterprise around
livelihoods and services. It is created in about two minutes — as fast as making an
instant coffee — and made discoverable to relevant peers across a district.
